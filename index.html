About Us

OxComposites is a society based at Oxford University, dedicated to exploring, designing, and building with composite materials. We focus on hands-on projects that bring together students from across the University who are passionate about engineering, innovation, and sustainability.

Projects

Our members collaborate on practical projects in areas such as aerospace, automotive, renewable energy, and sustainable materials. Project updates coming soon!
